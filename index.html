About Me

Hello! I am a website designer, builder, and SEO optimizer working in northwestern Wisconsin. I have almost 5 years of experience working within the website building industry and over 7 years of experience working in a professional and team environment. My passion for building websites began when I was in elementary school taking art classes. I learned I loved the creative side of art. However, I later realized I wanted something more structured as well. The motivation and desire to build beautiful, unique, user-friendly, SEO conscious websites have grown exponentially since college. I feel extremely lucky to say I truly love what I do!
