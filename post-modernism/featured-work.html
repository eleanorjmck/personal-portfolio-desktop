Varoom: The New

Marian Bantjes

Marian Bantjes is a Canadian artist who loves to work with a lot of bright colors. This art piece titled "Varoom: The New" was created in 2016 using pen, inks, and metallic tape. It is a hand-drawn illustration using bright colors with lettering, shapes, and special effects to make the artwork really stand out.
